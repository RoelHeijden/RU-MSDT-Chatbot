Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What should I do when I am looking for a book?

A: To find a book/journal you can make use of this link, which is the online database used by the Radboud University for study materials. If you want more information about how to use RU Quest, you can find the helpguide using this.

Q: How can I borrow a book and for how long?

A: All information about borrowing and/or reservations of publications, loan period and pick up times you can find here.

Q: Can you still borrow a book if you are not a RU student?

A: It is possible to use the library even if you are not a student at the Radboud University. More information about this can be found here.

Q: Is it possible to study in the library?

A: If you are a student of the Radboud University, you can book a workspace directly using this. If you are not, or you want more information about rules for reserving a workspace, you can use this.

Q: Can I access the library's databases off-campus?

A: A possible workaround to access the databases off-campus is to install the eduVPN. You can find more information here.

Q: What are the opening hours of the Central Library?

A: The Central Library is open on weekdays from 08:30 AM till 6:00 PM. On saturdays and sundays the Central Library is open from 10:00 AM till 6:00 PM. If you want to know more about the Central Library, click on this.

Help: how to use this chatbot

To make use of this chatbot optimally, there are a few things you can do.

  • If you have a question, click on one of the presented questions or just type your question.

  • Use the "Yes" and "No" buttons to provide feedback about the chatbot. This will help improving the replies of the chatbot.

  • If the chatbot cannot answer your question correctly, you can press the "No" button in the response of the chatbot that states "Have I answered your question?" after it has tried to answer your question. Then the chatbot will ask several questions, such as your surname and your emailaddress that are needed to make sure that you will get a response to your own emailaddress.

  • If you like to converse in another language, such as Dutch, you can select the "Change Language" option in the menu presented to the right of the name of the chatbot. Here you can select your prefered language. If the chatbot understands the selected language, it will start a new chat in this language. If it does not know the language, then it will inform you on that and ask if you do not mind to chat with the chatbot in English.

  • To access this help screen, you can simply send "help" to the chatbot and it will give a short summary of this page and a button that will sent you to this help page.
